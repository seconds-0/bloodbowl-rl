# BB2025 Skills & Traits — complete catalogue

Source: `docs/vendor/bloodbowlbase/bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/core_rules/skills_and_traits/index.html`
(errata-current as of May 2026). Annotations are as printed: **(ACTIVE)** / **(PASSIVE)**,
`*` = compulsory use, **ELITE** = Elite Skill (Matched Play: max 4 purchases each per team).

General rules: an ACTIVE skill works only while the player is Standing and not Distracted
(exceptions stated in the skill text, e.g. Jump Up works while Prone). A PASSIVE skill is
always in effect. Skill modifiers may be applied after the dice are rolled. Use is optional
unless starred. Multiple players may use the same skill per turn, and a player may use a
skill multiple times per turn, unless the skill says otherwise.

## BB2020 names you will NOT find here

Running Pass (→ Give and Go), Wildly Inaccurate (removed), Deflection (removed),
Pass Block (gone since BB2020; On the Ball is the replacement), Piling On (BB2016;
Pile Driver is the modern fouling cousin), Side Step (spelled Sidestep),
Blood Lust (now Bloodlust (X+)), Plague Ridden unchanged, Swarming is a *team special
rule* (Snotling), not a trait.
